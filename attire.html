Dress Code: Semi-Formal/Cocktail

Our wedding will be a beautiful outdoor celebration. We request semi-formal/cocktail attire that is elegant yet comfortable for an outdoor setting.

Women's Attire

We recommend cocktail dresses, elegant jumpsuits, or dressy separates. Our color palette is muted and earthy, so feel free to incorporate these tones.

Do's:

  • Cocktail or tea-length dresses
  • Elegant jumpsuits
  • Light, breathable fabrics
  • Wedges or block heels (for grass areas)

Don'ts:

  • All white or ivory outfits (reserved for the bride)
  • Casual sundresses or beachwear
  • Stiletto heels (venue has grass areas)

Men's Attire

We recommend dress pants with a button-up shirt and tie or a suit without a tie. Jackets are optional depending on the weather.

Do's:

  • Dress slacks with a button-up shirt
  • Ties in complementary colors
  • Suits in navy, gray, or neutral tones
  • Dress shoes (loafers or oxfords)

Don'ts:

  • Jeans or casual pants
  • Sneakers or sandals
  • T-shirts or polo shirts

Children's Attire

Children are welcome to dress in comfortable semi-formal attire. We understand comfort is key for the little ones!

Do's:

  • Comfortable dress clothes
  • Dress shoes or nice sandals
  • Clothes in wedding colors if desired

Don'ts:

  • Uncomfortable formal wear
  • Athletic shoes or flip flops

Important Notes

Weather Considerations: Garden party. Think light, breezy, and celebratory!
Venue Terrain: Please note that the events will take place outdoors, which includes lawn games! We recommend wearing comfortable shoes (e.g., block heels or flats) suitable for grass or uneven ground
